Our Branches

Immerse yourself in the allure of our hotel brand's various branches, each a distinct world waiting to be explored. We invite you to journey through our collection, where every branch tells a story of its own. Whether you yearn for the tranquility of a countryside escape, the vibrant city life, or the serene coastal breeze, our diverse branches cater to your every preference.

Each of our branches boasts a character that is as unique as its location. From the elegance of our City Center Branch to the tropical paradise of our Beachfront Resort Branch, we promise an unforgettable experience. Savor delectable cuisine, bask in luxurious amenities, and embrace the culture of each locale. Be it adventure, romance, or relaxation, our branches promise to enchant and fulfill your desires. So, select your destination and embark on a journey with us; let us be your gateway to a world of luxury, beauty, and extraordinary hospitality.

Luxe Horizon Hotel

Nestled along the pristine shores of a tropical island, our Beachfront Paradise Branch offers guests an unparalleled beachfront experience. Enjoy breathtaking ocean views from your private balcony, savor delectable seafood cuisine at our seaside restaurant, and unwind with spa treatments while listening to the gentle lull of the waves.

Location: Colombo 3

Urban Oasis Hotel

Located in the heart of a bustling metropolis, our Urban Oasis Branch caters to the modern traveler. With its sleek, contemporary design and proximity to the city's cultural attractions, it's the perfect choice for those seeking a vibrant urban experience. Explore the nearby museums, theaters, and gourmet dining options during your stay.

Location: Kandy

Mountain Retreat Hotel

Escape to the serenity of the mountains at our Mountain Retreat Branch. Surrounded by lush forests and towering peaks, this branch offers cozy cabins, hiking trails, and opportunities for wildlife spotting. It's an ideal destination for nature enthusiasts and those looking to unwind in a tranquil setting.

Location: Badulla

Historic Elegance Hotel

Immerse yourself in history and luxury at our Historic Elegance Branch. Housed in a meticulously restored heritage building, this branch exudes old-world charm. Explore the nearby landmarks, dine in an elegant, historic setting, and enjoy the timeless beauty of this unique destination.

Location: Anuradhapura

Tropical Hideaway Hotel

Tucked away on a secluded island, our Tropical Hideaway Branch offers seclusion and tranquility. You'll find overwater bungalows, crystal-clear lagoons, and lush gardens. It's the perfect choice for honeymooners and anyone seeking a romantic getaway in paradise.

Location: Awissawella
